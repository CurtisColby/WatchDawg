Public login: public / watchdawg

🍪 YouTube Cookies

Checking…
⚠️ YouTube resolves auto-paused — cookies are stale. Refresh cookies.txt below, then this clears itself on the next successful lookup.
Why: YouTube expires exported browser cookies every few weeks. When that happens, YouTube videos get stuck resolving or won't play, and the status above turns red. Refreshing takes two minutes:
  1. On a computer where you're logged into YouTube, go to youtube.com and export cookies with a cookie-export browser extension (Netscape format — e.g. “Get cookies.txt LOCALLY”). Export while on the YouTube tab.
  2. Copy the file onto the server:
    sudo cp ~/Downloads/www.youtube.com_cookies.txt ~/watchdawg-backend/config/cookies.txt
  3. Done — no restart needed. The status turns green on the next successful YouTube lookup (within one scheduler tick, or right away if you play a YouTube video).
To verify by hand from the server:
docker exec watchdawg-backend yt-dlp --cookies /config/cookies.txt --simulate --print title "https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jNQXAC9IVRw"
— it should print a video title, not a “Sign in to confirm you're not a bot” error.
